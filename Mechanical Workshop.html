Mechanical Workshop




A well-equipped mechanical workshop is currently being established at the Faculty of Technology. The mechanical workshop is equipped with a variety of machines namely lathe machines, milling machines, different types of computer numerical controlled (CNC) machines, woodworking machines, welding machines, hydraulic presses, laser cutters with metal cutting, surface grinders, shearing machines, rolling machines, band saws, tool grinders, and pedestal drills, etc. These tools and machinery are extensively utilized for teaching and research & development.
